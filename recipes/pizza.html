Pepperoni pizza recipe


Description

Make this homemade pizza with basic ingredients, then top it with any of your favorite toppings.


Ingredients

Dough

  • 500g wheat flour
  • 7g dry yeast
  • 312g lukewarm water
  • 6g sugar
  • 10g salt

Tomato sauce

  • 400g can peeled diced tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp sugar

Toppings

  • 150g shredded cheese
  • 150g sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Mix all dough ingredients
  2. Knead the dough for 20 minutes
  3. Form the dough into a ball and leave it to rise for an hour
  4. Mix all tomato sauce ingredients
  5. Blend the tomato sauce with a food processor
  6. Preheat the oven at max temperature
  7. Form a disc with the dough
  8. Spread the tomato sauce on the dough
  9. Add toppings, bake for 15 minutes
